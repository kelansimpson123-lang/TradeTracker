HOW TO SIZE A TRADE
1.Set your risk % (max 1-3% per day total)
2.Get SL distance in ticks from TradingView (below your entry to stop)
3.MES: ticks × $1.25 = cost per contract per tick
4.MNQ: ticks × $0.50 = cost per contract per tick
5.Contracts = $ Risk ÷ (ticks × tick value) — always round DOWN
6.Never exceed 3% total risk in a single day across all trades
THE TJR ICT SETUP
ENTRY CHECKLIST — STEP BY STEP
1
Mark Key Levels
PDH / PDL · Hourly unswept highs & lows · 30min session vertical lines + H/L · Asia High/Low
2
Identify HTF Liquidity Pool
Where is the nearest unswept LQ sitting above/below? Which pool is price most likely to draw to? This is your bias direction.
3
Wait for HTF Liquidity Sweep
Price must sweep the HTF LQ pool (PDH/PDL, hourly H/L, 30min session H/L). No sweep = no trade. Patience.
4
5 Minute BOS + Confluence
After the LQ sweep, wait for a clear 5min Break of Structure in the opposite direction. Must have a 5min confluence (IFVG, SMT, or strong displacement candle).
5
1 Minute BOS — Entry Trigger
Drop to 1min. Wait for the 1min BOS to confirm. This is your entry trigger. Do NOT enter before the 1min BOS.
6
Session Check
Only trade during London (03:00-08:00 EST) — Scenario 1 only. Or NY Open (09:30 EST onwards) — all scenarios. Never trade in between.
7
London End Check
If at end of London one H/L has been swept → wait for reversal. If NO sweep yet → wait for a level to get hit, then look for confluences going into NY.

Primary session. All 3 scenarios valid. Max 1-3% risk per day. Be at your desk ready.
⚠ LONDON TRADE RULE
London trades must be clean, early, and resolved well before 08:30 EST. New institutional money at Pre-Market open can invalidate your position. If you are not significantly in profit as Pre-Market approaches — close the trade. Never carry a London trade through the NY open under any circumstance.
NY SESSION SCENARIOS
Scenario 1
NY consolidates→London consolidates→NY sweeps LQ→NY reversal
Scenario 2
NY consolidates→London sweeps LQ→NY reversal from London sweep
Scenario 3
NY consolidates→London sweeps & reverses→NY continues London direction
⚡ If NY opens near opposing LQ — reassess for fresh manipulation & reversal instead of forcing continuation. Wait. Be patient. The setup comes to you.
