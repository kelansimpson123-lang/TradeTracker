How to Size a Trade — Step by Step
1.Check ForexFactory — red news within 5 mins of your setup? Wait or skip.
2.Identify your SL level (swing high/low of the leg). Measure distance in points on TradingView.
3.Dollar Risk = Account Balance × Risk% ÷ 100
4.Cost per contract = SL points × $2.00 (MNQ)
5.Contracts = Dollar Risk ÷ Cost per contract — always round DOWN.
6.Move to BE once price hits first internal high/low. Always take partial at 1:1 minimum.
7.1 trade taken today? Log it, close the platform, come back tomorrow.
⚡ Instrument Rule
09:30–11:00 EST — MNQ only for trades. MES for reference & SMT only. 1 trade per day max. No entry after 11:00 EST.
1
Mark Key Levels
PDH/PDL · Asia H/L · London H/L · Hourly unswept H/L · 30min session H/L · NDOG/NWOG · EQH/EQL/REQH/REQL · Data wicks (mark abnormal 1min wick highs/lows at 08:30 news)
2
Identify Draw on Liquidity (DOL)
External DOL: PDH/PDL, session H/L, weekly H/L, obvious swing H/L. Internal DOL: EQH/EQL, FVG, EQ (50%), ITH/ITL. Bias = which FVG is being respected vs disrespected. Market moves to: rebalance FVGs, seek equilibrium, or seek liquidity.
3
Wait for HTF Liquidity Sweep
Swing Low = red then green candle. Swing High = green then red candle. Colour doesn't matter in the 3-candle pattern. No sweep = no trade. Once swept, that level is no longer valid.
4
Identify the Leg & Highest TF IFVG
Leg = swing high to low (or low to high) that HIT the key level. On 1min, move UP timeframes until no bearish FVG exists (for longs). Take HIGHEST TF IFVG — higher TF holds higher power. All stacked FVGs must be inverted first.
5
FVG Timeframe Selection
1min IFVG — only if no 2/3/4/5min exists. 2min — only if no 3/4/5min. 3min — only if no 4/5min. Wait for CANDLE BODY CLOSE through the IFVG on that timeframe. Wicks can pass — body decides. FVG still valid until body closes through it.
6
Entry & Stop Loss
Enter on close of candle that inverts the IFVG. SL: at swing high/low of the leg. Fat move? SL at edge of FVG within leg. 0.5–1% risk max. MNQ: SL points × $2.00 × contracts = total dollar risk.
7
Targets & Management
Target 1:1 to 1:3 RR. Only target what you're highly confident price WILL hit — not swing traders. Move to BE when price hits first internal H/L. Always take profit at 1:1 minimum. Pro tip: equal lows below = don't go long. Equal highs above = don't go short.
SMT Divergence
Bullish SMT
NQ does NOT hit key SSL low → ES DOES hit same SSL level and rallies (or vice versa) → divergence = sign of reversal. NQ low is now a protected low. Look for IFVG entry targeting BSL.
Bearish SMT
ES does NOT hit BSL high → NQ DOES hit same BSL and reverses lower (or vice versa) → divergence = reversal confirmation. Only valid at MAJOR HTF swing H/L. Don't mark every SMT.
SMT Rules
Only at HTF key levels. Must align with HTF bias. Same time on both charts. Confirmation only — not required, but adds conviction. Always check MES when NQ gives a setup.
Market Structure
Swing High: green then red. Swing Low: red then green.
MSS: uptrend + body close BELOW most recent low = MSS down. Downtrend + body close ABOVE = MSS up. Fake MSS = LQ sweep. LTF going opposite to HTF = manipulation.
Liquidity
BSL: resting at swing highs (lower high → high → lower high).
SSL: resting at swing lows (low → lower low → low).
Colour doesn't matter in 3-candle pattern. Major ranges = 15min+. Once swept = no longer valid.
FVG — Fair Value Gap
Imbalance in price. Acts as magnet. Respected: wicks hold (can pass through). Disrespected: body closes through. IFVG: inverted = entry signal. Unmitigated = not yet traded into. Every FVG matters regardless of size. Mark 1min to Daily.
Equilibrium
EQ = 50% of the range.
Premium: above 50% = shorts.
Discount: below 50% = longs.
Price retraces into discount FVGs before continuing. Mark on 5min+ and big impulse moves only. Trades don't HAVE to be in discount but price should at least retrace there.
Internal vs External DOL
External: outside the range — PDH/PDL, session H/L, weekly H/L, obvious swing H/L.
Internal: inside the range — EQH/EQL/REQH/REQL, FVG, EQ (50%), internal swing H/L.
Internal gets hit first before external DOL is reached.
ITH / ITL
Intermediate High/Low: a H/L resting WITHIN a FVG. Below bullish FVG or above bearish FVG — only valid while FVG holds (wick, not body). Great rejection level AND target. Zoom into 15min ITH within 4hr FVG for precise entry.
EQH / EQL / REQH / REQL
REQL: failed sweeps at same low = strong draw.
High resistance: price sweeps past most recent H/L.
Low resistance: fails to push past.
SL goes at high resistance. Target low resistance liquidity. Great targets when aligning with HTF bias.
NDOG / NWOG
New Day Opening Gap: created at 6pm EST.
New Week Opening Gap: very strong draw on LQ.
Price may just tap in — doesn't need to fill completely. Mark the edge. Unfilled 5min/15min FVGs work similarly as strong internal DOL.
Data Wicks
Strong 1min wick at 08:30 news (CPI/PPI/NFP) = data high or low. Mark the abnormal wick level — strong DOL once price retraces. Example: big 1min up at 08:30 → mark data high → price retraces into bullish FVG → targets data high.
Key times: 08:30 / 09:45 / 10:00 / 14:00.

True Day Open
True day open = 6pm EST. OHLC = red candle (Open High Low Close). OLHC = green candle (Open Low High Close). Every timeframe is fractal. Price always does one of three things: rebalance FVGs, seek equilibrium, or seek liquidity.
The 3 Questions — Ask Every Morning Before the Session
1. Are we delivering from BSL or SSL? Where has price moved FROM — buy side or sell side LQ taken?
2. Which FVG are we respecting vs disrespecting? Respecting bullish FVG = bullish bias. Disrespecting bearish = bullish bias.
3. What external & internal draws align with my bias? External: PDH/PDL, session H/L. Internal: EQH/EQL, FVG, EQ 50%.
IF/THEN Examples
Bullish Example
Swept PDL → ITL inside daily BISI (bullish FVG) → displacing higher. IF 15min + hourly CISD THEN expect London High + recent BSL as external draws. Data highs at AM open = internal draw.
Bearish Example
Price not filling NWOG, running toward BSL = Asia Judas. Lean bearish. Trading inside 30min BISI. IF disrespected THEN completely bearish toward NWOG. Bullish until proven bearish — conditions set the switch.
Neutral Example
Range bound. 4hr sell model. Lean bearish IF 09:30 Judas higher toward PDH + daily FVG. 1hr gap = condition. IF respected → longs to PDH. IF disrespected → bearish toward REQL.
📌 "Bullish until proven bearish at all time highs." Write your IF/THEN BEFORE the session opens. The setup comes to you — you don't chase it.
